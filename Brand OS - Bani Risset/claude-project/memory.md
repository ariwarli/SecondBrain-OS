**Key learnings & principles**

- **Positioning**: Bani's credibility is significantly richer than what has historically been visible on his profiles; closing the gap between real track record and perceived positioning is an ongoing priority (e.g., MDG Award, UN Agency work, 10-year Australian client retention were underrepresented).
- **Licensing economics matter**: Platform decisions (e.g., CloudChat.id vs. WhatsCRM) hinge on per-client licensing costs at scale — always model multi-client scenarios, not single-project costs.
- **"1000+ clients" framing**: The accurate, defensible claim is "Dipercaya 1000+ clients" — inclusive of corporate clients, individual buyers, training participants, and e-commerce transactions across 18 years. "1000+ bisnis" is too narrow.
- **Content before perfection**: Post before perfecting; iterate based on engagement data.
- **Revenue model clarity**: Project-based micro-consulting with business owners as primary audience; avoid scope creep into service delivery before payment is confirmed.
- **Pomodoro/focus**: Bani is in early stages of building deep work habits; success is defined by consistency over volume (even one uninterrupted session counts).
