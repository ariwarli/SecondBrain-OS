## Problem Statement

Founders, CEO, dan Strategic Consultant dibombardir informasi setiap hari (meeting, podcast, artikel, newsletter). Masalahnya:
1. **Information Leakage:** Ide bagus hilang karena tidak sempat dicatat dengan benar.
2. **Context Fragmentation:** Catatan tersebar di WhatsApp, Notion, Google Docs, dan otak.
3. **Retrieval Failure:** Saat butuh data untuk ambil keputusan, mereka lupa di mana menyimpannya atau apa detailnya.
4. **Content Friction:** Sulit memproduksi konten otoritas (Authority Architecture) karena harus mulai dari nol setiap kali.

---

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Founders & CEO (High-leverage decision makers) yang sibuk.
- **Secondary:** Strategic Consultants & Coaches yang membangun personal brand.
- **Tertiary:** Knowledge Workers yang ambisius (ADHD thinkers, polymaths).

---

## Core Value Proposition

> "Ubah overload informasi menjadi senjata strategis. Tangkap ide lewat Telegram, simpan di Obsidian, dan tanya database pengetahuan Anda menggunakan AI."

Privacy-first. Mobile-native capture. AI-powered retrieval. Source-cited answers.

---

## Competitive Positioning

| Kompetitor | Kelemahan yang diisi SBF |
|---|---|
| Notion Second Brain Templates | Too heavy, high friction to capture on mobile, poor AI retrieval logic. |
| Tiago Forte's Building a Second Brain | Framework only, no automated AI implementation. |
| ChatGPT / Perplexity | General knowledge only, not based on YOUR private insights/notes. |
| Mem.ai | Black-box, proprietary storage, difficult to export/own. |

**Unique angle:** Obsidian-First (Data Ownership) + Telegram-Native (Low Friction) + Private LLM/Vector Integration (Contextual Intelligence).

---

## Scope Decisions

### V1 — In Scope (The "Founder's Kit")
- **Capture Layer:** Telegram Bot setup (Text, Voice-to-Text).
- **Storage Layer:** Obsidian Vault Template (PARA + Authority Architecture).
- **Retrieval Layer:** Simple Python/Node script for Vector Indexing (Local/Cloud).
- **Answer Layer:** Query interface via Telegram (Ask your Second Brain).
- **Automation:** Auto-classification of notes (Project vs Resource vs Reference).

### V1 — Out of Scope (Future Roadmap)
- Multi-user collaboration (Enterprise version).
- Full web-UI for database management.
- Integration with 100+ apps (Keep it lean with Telegram/Webhook).
- Fine-tuned local model hosting (Initially use API providers for speed).
